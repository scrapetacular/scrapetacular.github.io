SCRAPEtacular (Beta)

A simple way of performing web scraping and data extraction to CSV and XLS format.

Welcome

SCRAPEtacular is a service for extracting structured data from most websites [1] into a spreadsheet or database, without writing any code. You can use this for Data Science, Recruitment, Market Research, Real Estate, or anywhere you would normally build a data-set online, before performing data analysis in Excel or R.

How do I scrape a website with this?

Just type in a few examples of the data you want to extract, and in a few seconds, the advanced machine learning algorithms [2] will do the rest.
